üìä Analytics That Actually Work Now

Remember when your Tuesday workout showed up on Wednesday? Or when clicking a heat map square went nowhere? Yeah, we fixed all that.

  • Timezone-Aware Date Aggregation: Analytics now convert UTC timestamps to YOUR timezone before grouping workouts by day. If you lift at 11pm EST on Tuesday, it shows up on Tuesday ‚Äì not Wednesday UTC.
  • Heat Map Date Display Fixed: Hovering over workout squares now shows the correct day. No more off-by-one errors from timezone-naive date parsing.
  • Real Workout IDs in Heat Maps: Clicking a heat map square now navigates to the actual workout. AI was generating fake IDs before (like "workout_summary_2025-10-18"). Now it uses real ones.
  • Workout Date Validation: Backend now catches and corrects workout dates with wrong years (like 2024 instead of 2025). Stops bad data at the source.
  • Efficient Data Transfer: Analytics queries now fetch only summary fields instead of full workout objects. 70-90% less data transfer, way faster load times.
Why this matters: Your training data is the foundation of everything your coaches do. If dates are wrong, trends are wrong. If links break, you lose context. We tightened this up hard.
