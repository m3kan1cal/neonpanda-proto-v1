My Recommendation: Option 1 (Compact Horizontal)

Why: It strikes the best balance between space efficiency and maintaining the coach relationship. The coach remains present and interactive, but doesn't dominate the page. The horizontal layout is more scannable and modern.

Key Improvements:

  • Saves ~150-200px of vertical space vs. current design
  • Coach card becomes clickable/interactive (opens chat or coach profile)
  • Command palette integrated as a button rather than taking a full line
  • Removes redundant "track progress" description text
  • Creates clear visual hierarchy: Page ID ‚Üí Coach ‚Üí Actions
  • Aligns with modern dashboard/SaaS UI patterns

Consider Option 3 if: You want to really emphasize the coach relationship and have quick actions always visible (great for desktop-first experience).
