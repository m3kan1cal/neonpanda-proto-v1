📅 Training Programs Are Here

Your coach can now build complete multi-week training plans – when you need them.

Some of you follow a box program. Some work with a personal trainer and just want to log workouts for accountability. Some are training for a Spartan race or 5K. Some train intuitively. Some just want a coach to talk to about today's workout. That's all perfect – NeonPanda works however you train.

But for those times when you need a structured plan – getting ready for the 2026 CrossFit Open, prepping for a powerlifting meet, building a strength cycle, training for an endurance event – your coaches can now build complete programs through conversation. No other AI coaching platform can do this.

🎯 When You Need Structure, It's There

Training Programs are intelligent systems for when you want:

  • Multi-week progression toward a specific goal (competition prep, strength cycles, etc.)
  • Phase-based periodization with strategic progression and deloads
  • A coach who knows where you're headed long-term, not just what you're doing today
  • Calendar-based accountability that adapts when life gets chaotic

But if you don't need this? Keep logging workouts and chatting with your coach like always. Both approaches work beautifully.

NEW FEATURE

💬 Build Mode - Talk Your Way to a Program

Forget forms. Forget templates. Just talk to your coach about what you want to achieve, and they'll build a complete training program through conversation.

Here's how it works:

  • Switch to Build Mode in any conversation with the new purple toggle
  • Tell your coach your goal: "I want to build strength for the next 8 weeks" or "Need a program to prep for my first powerlifting meet"
  • Answer their questions naturally: They'll ask about your schedule, equipment, experience, constraints – just like a real coach would
  • Get a complete program: Your coach generates a structured plan with phases, progression, and daily workout templates written in plain English

No forms. No dropdowns. Just conversation. That's the NeonPanda way.

PHASE 1: FOUNDATION

⚡ What Shipped This Week

This release is Phase 1 – the infrastructure and ability to create training programs through conversation. You can build complete programs with your coach right now. Phase 2 (viewing your program calendar, tracking adherence, logging from templates) ships next.

Behind the scenes, we built:

  • Smart calendar generation: Your program knows "today's workout" based on YOUR timezone, not server time. Handles rest days, pauses, and real-world schedule chaos.
  • Phase-aware AI: Your coach understands where you are in the program and what comes next. They can intelligently adapt workouts based on your current phase goals.
  • Semantic search integration: Training programs get stored in Pinecone so your coach can reference past programs, learn from what worked, and avoid what didn't.
  • Async generation: Program building happens in the background while your coach keeps you updated with real-time progress messages. No waiting around.

You just see a smooth conversation. We see hundreds of hours of engineering that made it feel effortless.
