NEW

Training Pulse — See Your Progress Like Never Before

We built a complete visual analytics dashboard. Not a basic chart or two — a full training intelligence center that turns your workout data into insights you can actually use.

  • Volume trend charts: Watch your total tonnage evolve over time — see the trajectory of your work
  • Strength curves with PR markers: Deep dive into any exercise and see exactly where your PRs happened
  • PR timeline: Track personal records progression across your entire history
  • Body balance radar chart: Visualize your movement patterns across squat, hinge, push, pull, carry, and core — spot imbalances before they become problems
  • Recovery load tracking: Acute:chronic workload ratio so you can train hard without overtraining
  • Weekly comparisons: This week vs last week vs your 4-week average — are you progressing or plateauing?
  • Sparkline mini-trends: Compact trend lines embedded right in your streak and PR cards
  • Flexible time ranges: Zoom in (4 weeks) or zoom out (1 year) with a single click

Find it at Training Grounds → Training Pulse. Every chart is styled in our synthwave theme because your data should look as good as your lifts.

NEW

Share Your Training Programs With Anyone

Built a killer program? Now you can share it. Create a link, send it to a friend, training partner, or client — they can preview the full program and copy it into their own account with one click.

  • Shareable links: Generate a public link for any training program you've built
  • Public preview: Anyone can view the program details — no account required to browse
  • One-click copy: Recipients copy the program into their account and choose which coach to pair it with
  • Manage your shares: Dedicated page in Training Grounds to see all your shared programs

Whether you're a coach sharing programming with athletes, or you just want to show your training buddy what you've been running — it's now as easy as sending a link.

REBUILT

Your Coach Now Knows Everything

This is the big one. We completely rebuilt how your AI coach works. It's no longer just answering questions in isolation — it now has full cross-context awareness across your entire training history.

  • Searches past conversations — your coach can recall what you've discussed before, across any session
  • Queries your memories — preferences, injuries, goals, and constraints are all retrievable in real-time
  • Browses your programs — checks active and completed programs, phases, adherence rates, and today's prescribed workout
  • Pulls exercise history — looks up PRs, strength trends, and volume for any exercise across all your workouts
  • Logs multiple workouts at once — tell your coach about many workouts in the same message and it handles them in parallel
  • Knows today's workout — asks "what's my workout today?" and your coach pulls it from your active program
  • Advances your program — log a workout and mark it complete on your program in the same conversation
  • Searches the methodology library — 38+ training methodologies available for evidence-based coaching advice
  • Remembers what you tell it — say "remember that I tweaked my knee" and it's stored for future sessions

11+ tools powering agentic AI with real reasoning — it decides what to look up, when to look it up, and how to use it. Your coach just went from smart to genuinely aware.

UPGRADED

Smarter Workout Logging — Just Tell Your Coach What You Did

We rebuilt the entire workout logging pipeline from the ground up. It's powered by 10+ tools that work together so the system can make real decisions — what to extract, how to validate, and when to save — with much higher accuracy.

Auto-Detect

Automatically identifies your discipline — powerlifting, CrossFit, running, bodybuilding, and more.

Full Extraction

Exercises, sets, reps, weights, duration, rest periods — all extracted from natural language.

AI Validation

Checks completeness and confidence before saving. If the data isn't right, it won't save incomplete workouts.

Smart Normalization

Corrects issues, applies defaults, ensures your data is clean and consistent every time.

Plus: multi-workout support (log many workouts in a single message) and program linking (logged workouts automatically connect to your active program).

REBUILT

Coach Creator & Program Designer — Smarter Flows, Better Results

Both the Coach Creator and Program Designer have been migrated to our new conversation agent architecture. What does that mean for you?

  • Agentic memory: The Coach Creator remembers everything you've said across the entire conversation — no more repeating yourself
  • Natural conversations: Questions adapt based on what you've already shared, not a rigid script
  • Smarter program generation: The Program Designer now has deeper context about your goals, constraints, and training history
  • Training directives: Set a global training focus and every coach interaction respects it

If you haven't built a new coach or designed a program recently — try it now. The difference is night and day.

Explain API: In the Program Designer, workout terms (exercises, set schemes, methodology jargon) are explained dynamically using AI — hover or tap to get instant, contextual definitions so you can learn as you build.
